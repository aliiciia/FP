Personal & proffesional Background

The Megamindz is a company dedicated to music production, audio engineering and sound design. We are currently located in Orlando Florida but thanks to transportation, we can be worldwide. our passion for music includes producing, recording and tracking, editing, mixing, and live recording.


The Megamindz is a growing team that can get the job done correctly and on time. We do not offer anything less than industry quality, which has gotten us in contact to many of your favorite artist and producers.


We offer instrumentals right here than you can purchase. We also make custom instrumentals for everyone that would prefer something new. We currently produce in just about every genre.


The Megamindz were established in 2008 in Orlando Florida with nothing more than a broken down desktop. Now we have grown and have many professional equipment like the Roland Fantom, Bx8a monitors, Novation midi controller, Native Instruments Machine plus many more. As of software we use Native Instruments products, Spectrasonics, Refx, and many more. With all this equipment we promise to produce nothing but the best.
